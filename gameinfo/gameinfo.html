Pet Clickers is developed by a team of three avid Game players. Together we have 70 years of active game playing. Since we all love spending crazy amounts of time playing different games we think we know something about how a good game should be. We decided to build a game we like to play.

And here it is Pet Clickers!!

🐕🐾 Your Dream Pet Awaits: Imagine a world where adorable pets come to life – from loyal dogs and mischievous cats to cunning foxes and majestic elephants. With stunning visuals and charming animations, "Pet Clickers" brings your dream of pet companionship to your fingertips!




🌈🎁 Surprises Await: Hidden treasures, delightful interactions, and unexpected surprises await as you explore the different worlds of "Pet Clickers." Every moment is a chance to discover something new and exciting!




🐾🐣 The Adventure Begins Soon: Get ready to embark on a journey of cuteness, challenges, and endless entertainment! "Pet Clickers" is just around the corner, so stay tuned for the official release announcement in late december.




🚀🔝 Endless Upgrades and Adventures: Prepare for a journey filled with upgrades and activities galore! Customize pet habitats, conquer exciting challenges, and embark on thrilling quests as you make your way through the enchanting world of "Pet Clickers." Each click takes you closer to an unforgettable experience!




🏆🌐 Connect, Compete, and Conquer: Forge connections with fellow players, engage in friendly competition through mini-games, and climb the leaderboards to showcase your pet-raising prowess. Will you be the top "Pet Clickers" contender?

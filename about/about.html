About us and the Company


We are a team of three avid Game players. Together we have 70 years of active game playing.
Since we all love spending crazy amounts of time playing different games we think we know
something about how a good game should be. One day we sat down together talking about what
to do when "we grow up". The imminent answer for all three of us was MAKE GAMES! This led
to the start up of this company. We have just started developing games.
The first code was written in june in 2023. And... Pet Clickers is developed by a team of three avid Game players. Together we have 70 years of active game playing. Since we all love spending crazy amounts of time playing different games we think we know something about how a good game should be. We decided to build a game we like to play. And here it is Pet Clickers!! When we started making our game Pet Clickers we had multiple meetings on WHAT we wanted our game to be, we came to the conclusion that a Pet simulator was a good choice as it is still popular in 2023; but we thought it would be a bit too stale and same-y so we discussed what else could be added... That's where we got the Clickers part. Cookie Clicker games were extremely popular but was a classic 2014-2019 genre. We mixed both of these popular genres to create a whole new yet familiar experience for you players!
